# هموم داعية — Concerns of a Preacher

Interactive trilingual web app based on **"Humum Da'iya"** (1983) by **Sheikh Mohammed al-Ghazali** (1917-1996).

Al-Ghazali's most personal book — his concerns about dawah, the Ummah, and the challenges facing Muslim preachers and communities.

## Features

- **20 Concern Cards**: Key issues from the book — secondary disputes, political despotism, women's rights, Palestine, intellectual stagnation, and more
- **8 Solutions**: Al-Ghazali's vision for reform — education, justice, unity, mercy in dawah
- **Scenario Quiz**: "What Would You Do?" — 10 real-life dawah scenarios with lifelines
- **Daily Reflections**: 12 reflections from the book with streak tracking
- **XP & Badges**: Gamification system with 6 badges (Beginner, Reader, Scholar, Persistent, Expert, Preacher)
- **Narrator**: Full audiobook narrator with karaoke, duo reading (AR+FR), sleep timer
- **Trilingual**: Arabic, English, French
- **3 Themes**: Earth (brown), Night (dark), Olive (green)
- **PWA**: Works offline, installable
